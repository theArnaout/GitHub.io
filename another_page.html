Other Page: More About Me

While attending school for Marketing I decided to become a personal trainer. I did that for a few years before going into personal training sales and then later moving into a district sales management role in retail. I also opened my own business making and selling fitness equipment.

Despite most of my adult job experience being outside of computers and programming, I was exposed to a fair bit of computers while growing up. When I was around eleven years old, I worked at a local computer repair shop during my summer break. During this period I attempted to make my own games but was not very successful.

The next time I fiddled with coding was when I started my business on Shopify. Shopify has many tools that make it easy to start a website without prior coding knowledge but, the ability to customize and actually make your website function the way you imagine was still difficult. So, I tried to learn different ways to do them manually. While only partially successful, it did give me a very basic taste of what coding was like.
